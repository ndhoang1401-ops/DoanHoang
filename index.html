Hi, I'm DoanHoang 🌸

"Design is not what it looks like — it's how it feels when someone actually uses it."
— My design philosophy

I'm a self-taught UI/UX Designer & Full-Stack Engineer from Vietnam 🇻🇳. My journey started in 2023 with nothing but curiosity and a browser — no bootcamp, no mentor, just relentless iteration. Today I design and ship end-to-end digital products that are not just functional, but genuinely feel inevitable to use.

My process starts at the intersection of user psychology and technical feasibility. I believe great interfaces should be invisible — guiding people effortlessly without them noticing the craft underneath. Every pixel, every transition, every line of code is a decision. I take all of them seriously.

Outside the screen, I train Calisthenics every day — consistency compounds, intensity is earned, and fundamentals never lie. Also deep-diving into System Design and Advanced Chemistry.

Calisthenics started the same day as coding — 2023. No gym, no equipment, nothing but bodyweight and discipline. Both journeys mirror each other: both reward consistency over intensity, both punish skipped fundamentals, and both show results that look sudden but took months of invisible progress.

2023 — Tháng 1–3

Ground Zero 💪

Couldn't do 5 consecutive pull-ups. Could barely hold a dead hang for 10 seconds. Started with negative pull-ups and Australian rows every single day. Same with coding: couldn't write a for-loop correctly. Both journeys began with embarrassingly basic foundations.

🔴 0 pull-ups🤝 First push-up form
2023 — Tháng 4–8

Foundation Phase — Building the Base

Hit 10 clean pull-ups. Dips became easy. Started ring work. The compound effect kicked in — every session felt like leveling up. Applied the same grind to JS: from "hello world" to building small full-stack apps in the same 5-month window.

🟡 10 pull-ups✅ Dips mastered💍 Ring support hold
2024 — Năm thứ hai

First Muscle-Up — The Milestone Everyone Talks About

Unlocked the muscle-up mid-2024. Failed hundreds of times before it clicked. The moment it worked felt identical to pushing my first app live on a real domain: sudden, unexpected, earned.

🏆 Muscle-up unlocked🌀 L-sit 10s🤸 Tuck planche
2025 — Hiện tại

Advanced Moves — Skill Work

Current focus: Handstand (freestanding 15+s), Front Lever progression (full tuck → advanced tuck), and ring muscle-ups. Training 6 days/week, 1 hour per session. No excuses accepted.

🌟 Handstand 15s📐 Front lever tuck💍 Ring MU🎯 6×/week

The lesson that transferred from calisthenics to code: there are no shortcuts, only compressions of time through better decisions.

Started in 2023 với zero background — không có bằng CS, không có mentor, không có roadmap. Chỉ có một browser, một text editor, và niềm tin rằng bất kỳ thứ gì cũng có thể học được nếu không chịu dừng lại.

2023

Những dòng code đầu tiên 💻

Bắt đầu với JavaScript từ YouTube và MDN docs. Không mentor, không bootcamp. Viết calculator, to-do list, clock — những thứ nhỏ nhất. Mỗi khi code chạy được, cảm giác không khác gì pull-up đầu tiên. Cùng lúc học Python và C++ song song vì tò mò.

🟡 JS cơ bản🐍 Python hello world📝 DOM manipulation
2024

Full-Stack đầu tiên — Node.js + PostgreSQL

Build app đầu tiên có database thật, authentication, REST API. Deploy lên Render — site live 2 tiếng rồi crash vì config sai. Sửa đến 2 giờ sáng. Đó là cái đêm hiểu ra rằng "biết code" và "biết ship" là hai thứ khác hoàn toàn.

🟢 Node.js + Express🗄️ PostgreSQL🚀 First deploy
2025

React + Animation — Tìm thấy UI/UX

Chuyển sang React, học GSAP, Three.js, glassmorphism. Nhận ra mình không chỉ thích code — mình thích làm cho thứ gì đó đẹp và mượt. UI/UX trở thành phần không thể tách rời. Bắt đầu obsess với Lighthouse score, performance, accessibility.

⚛️ React ecosystem✨ GSAP animations🎨 UI/UX mindset
2026

Ship thật — skr-hub, portfolio này và tiếp theo

Shipped skr-hub (Lighthouse 95+), portfolio này với 2000+ dòng hand-written HTML/CSS/JS. Đang học System Design và kết hợp Advanced Chemistry để build educational simulations. Mục tiêu: một sản phẩm có real users.

🏆 Lighthouse 95+🏗️ System Design⚗️ Chemistry × Code
